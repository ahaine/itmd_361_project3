Hobbies

Video Games & Streaming

Everyone has their own hobbies that they enjoy. For some, that might be baseball, football, fishing, or drawing. If those hobbies are not on your list, I aplogize. The hobbies I listed are the only examples I could think of.

For me, I enjoy videogames, music, and streaming!

I might of caught you off guard or confused you when I said streaming.. Let me explain. I recently developed my own brand and started streaming myself playing video games. I do not get many viewers, but money is not the goal. I just ike the idea of creating funny live streams and hanging out with other people who love video games. My love for video games started at a young age. The very first game I remember playing is Pokemon Yellow for the Gameboy Color. I grew up on Nintendo games and eventually switched over to PC. One of my favorite games to this day is Super Smash Bros for the N64 Console.

Sorry, I went off on a tangent. That is what happens when you love something so much and you can't withold your excitement.

Drumming and listening to music has helped me get through a lot of struggles in life.
